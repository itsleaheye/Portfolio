Where It All Started

I have always been a logic and technology orientated individual with a very analytical mindset. Due to this, I have always excelled in technical classes such as: mathematics, physics, computer sciences, etc. I have also had several people tell me that it's ironic that I chose to explore computer software developement, given that both of my parents work in the field as well (my mother is a systems analyst, and my father a software engineer). So there has been a long time running joke that it was my legacy to also end up a strong developer. I like to think though that the reason I am such a "tech-savy" individual is due to the fact that I have been expanding my coding skills since early 2013. Specifically it all started when I had been invited to an exclusive advanced placement program for students to learn coding (junior high students were invited to take high school level software courses). From that point onwards, I was always actively broadening my developement skills (enrolled in coding summer camps, computer science courses -jr.high and highschool-, learning through informal methods, etc.) So it is safe to say that I have always had a love for technology, video games, and anything related to computers. This has created a strong passion for code developement and software testing, and it has become my dream to work with computers.
