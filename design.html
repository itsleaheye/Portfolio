Illustration


I have been running and managing my own freelance digital artwork business since early 2013. Originally, it had started out with the intention to only create small character illustrations as a hobby. However, over the years my design skills and the hardware I use (a Wacom Intuos Pro), have evolved to allow me to expand my business to the point I can now offer character design consultations, illustration services, etc. Additionally, all digital artwork skills that I currently have, were purely obtained through informal learning means, and self-direction.

Software Used: Adobe Creative Cloud, Adobe Photoshop CC (2014-2020), Adobe Light Room, and Adobe Premiere Elements (2014)

Graphic Design


Around the same time that I launched my freelance digital artist career, I started to explore the world of graphic design. In addition to this, I also began to explore the fundamental design rules that influene our day to day life (i.e. marketing, etc). As my informal and formal knowledge surrounding the design world grew, my style evolved over the years with it -into one of a more minimalist nature-. However, I typically tend to create logos, or banners that feature some sort of an illustrative element.

Software Used: Adobe Creative Cloud, Adobe Photoshop CC (2014-2020), and Adobe Lightroom
